# Multi Timeframe POC Indicator

## Overview
The Multi Timeframe POC (Point of Control) indicator is designed to provide insights into market structure by analyzing price action across multiple timeframes. This indicator aims to help traders identify key support and resistance levels based on volume distribution.

## Key Features
- Analyze and display POC levels for different timeframes.
- Customizable settings to suit various trading strategies.
- Visual representation of POC levels on price charts.

## Installation
1. Download the indicator script from the repository.
2. Import the script into your trading platform (e.g., MetaTrader, TradingView).
3. Apply the indicator to your charts and configure settings as per your trading needs.

## Usage
1. **Timeframe Adjustment**: Select the timeframes for which you want to calculate POC. You can choose multiple timeframes simultaneously.
2. **Custom Settings**: Adjust parameters such as length, standard deviation, and alerts.
3. **Interpretation**: POC levels are displayed on the chart. Look for areas where the POC coincides with price action for potential trading opportunities.

## Conclusion
The Multi Timeframe POC indicator is a powerful tool for enhancing your trading decisions. By considering multiple timeframes and volume data, you can better understand market dynamics and increase your chances of success.
